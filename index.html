About me

Designing in my passion

I was introduced to design by a friend who loved drawing cartoon characters. As for me, I was more interested in sketching buildings. Surprisingly, I chose to study graphic design instead of architecture—just to see if it suited me. Gradually, with each drawing and every poster I created, I found myself falling a little more in love with this field.

I’m currently studying at Arena Multimedia, a place that not only teaches me about design but also helped me discover my second passion: photography.
